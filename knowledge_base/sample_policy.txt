Company Refund Policy

1. All purchases are eligible for a full refund within 30 days of the original purchase date.
2. To request a refund, contact our support team at support@example.com with your order number.
3. Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after approval.
4. Digital products are eligible for refund only if they have not been downloaded or accessed.
5. Shipping costs are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error.
6. Items must be returned in their original packaging and in unused condition.
7. After 30 days, exchanges may be offered at the discretion of customer service.
8. Subscription services can be cancelled at any time, but partial month refunds are not provided.
9. Gift cards and promotional items are non-refundable.
10. For defective products, contact us immediately — we will replace or refund at no additional cost.
